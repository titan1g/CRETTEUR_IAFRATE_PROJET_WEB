Summons water droplets resembling musical notes that form a Melody Loop, dealing Hydro DMG to surrounding enemies and afflicting them with the Wet status. Melody Loop Barbara's Normal Attacks heal all party members and nearby allied characters for a certain amount of HP, which scales with Barbara's Max HP. Her Charged Attack generates 4 times the amount of healing. Regenerates a certain amount of the current character's HP at regular intervals.
